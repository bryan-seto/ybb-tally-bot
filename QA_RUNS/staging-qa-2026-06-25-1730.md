## Key Findings

### ЁЯФ┤ Blockers

**1. `/pending` and `/showAllPendingTransactions` crash every call (A-06, A-08)**
- Both return: *"Sorry, I encountered an error retrieving pending transactions."*
- Occurs while real unsettled transactions exist (balance = SGD $109.89 owed).
- `/history`, `/detailedBalance`, `/balance` all work тАФ issue is isolated to the pending handler query.
- Core feature broken for users.

### ЁЯЯа Majors

**2. AI Gemini free-tier quota exhausted mid-session (B-07)**
- After ~20 AI calls, bot replies: *"ЁЯЪл AI daily free limits ran out."*
- Affects any expense not handled by the quick parser (desc-first transport keywords, some FX combos, aliases like RINGGIT).
- Cascades into timeouts on B-22 and B-28.
- `grab 5` (B-07) specifically: `grab` in desc-first is not in the quick parser's transport keywords тАФ should be.

**3. `IDR 150000 gojek` hangs indefinitely (B-14)**
- Sends "тП│ Saving expense..." and never resolves. 48s timeout.
- All other FX currencies (VND, MYR, USD, JPY, THB, AUD) work. IDR is the outlier.
- Likely FX API rate lookup timeout for IDR.

**4. `RINGGIT 50 groceries` hangs indefinitely (B-22)**
- Sends "ЁЯСА Processing expense..." and never resolves. 48s timeout.
- `RM` alias works fine (B-20). `RINGGIT` is not in the quick parser alias map тЖТ hits AI тЖТ quota exhausted тЖТ hangs.
- Fix: add `RINGGIT` тЖТ `MYR` to quick parser alias map.

**5. Mixed FX+SGD multi-line batch hangs (B-28)**
- `VND 50000 pho\n15 grab` stuck 48s.
- Single-currency multi-line batches (B-25, B-26, B-32) all work perfectly.
- Root cause: `grab` in the batch triggers AI fallback тЖТ quota exhausted тЖТ entire batch hangs.
